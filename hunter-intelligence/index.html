Common questions
Do I need to share my wallet or private keys?
Never. Hunter is a pure intelligence product. You receive scored rankings, ROI calculations, and farming instructions. You execute in your own wallet. We never touch your funds or keys.
How is Hunter different from Telegram alpha groups?
Alpha groups give tips from humans with unknown motivations. Hunter applies a consistent, transparent 7-factor scoring model to every protocol, plus quantitative tools — ROI calculation, snapshot probability, correlation analysis, and institutional flow tracking — that no alpha group provides.
What is the snapshot probability score based on?
Four signals: on-chain activity patterns (unusual transaction spikes often precede snapshots), token contract deployment status, team communication analysis, and historical timing from comparable protocol launches. Combined into a single probability estimate updated weekly.
How are whale wallets identified?
Hunter curates addresses known to belong to sophisticated airdrop farmers based on their historical on-chain activity — wallets that have consistently received significant airdrop allocations. All activity is publicly visible on-chain. We observe and report; we never interact with those wallets.
